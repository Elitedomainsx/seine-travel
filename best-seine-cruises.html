Champagne Tasting Cruise

Best for: Couples and romantic dates.

Why choose it: It feels more intimate and refined than a standard group cruise without becoming a full dinner plan.

Main downside: It is a lighter experience, so it is not the best fit if you want a full meal or a long ride.

Check availability and prices

This one works best when you care more about mood and exclusivity than ticking off every monument with commentary. The private lounge setting and sommelier-led tasting make it feel more special than a standard sightseeing cruise.

  • Tasting of 3 French champagnes, often including Brut and Rosé
  • Private salon atmosphere instead of a standard open group setup
  • Sommelier guidance adds more structure to the experience
  • Better for a date or celebration than for families or non-drinkers

Guests usually praise the lounge vibe and the sommelier’s explanations more than the sightseeing itself.
